WHAT IS SKYPE

Skype is a software program that allows you to make and receive calls. You can hear and see the person you are talking to via video chat.

HOW IT WORKS

When teaching on Skype you can see and hear your teacher just like in a normal classroom. You can listen to his/her pronunciation, gestures and facial expressions. In the Skype chat at the bottom of the page you can communicate with your teacher in writing. Your teacher can write down difficult words or you can send him/her words or sentences.
